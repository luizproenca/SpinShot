Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

SpinShot 360 values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: name, email address, and account information.
  • Content created in the app: generated videos, uploaded images, custom frames, and related files.
  • Usage data: information about feature usage, app interactions, and service performance.

2. Device Permissions

  • Camera: used to record videos inside the app.
  • Microphone: used to capture audio while recording videos.
  • Photo library: used to select existing media and save generated videos to your device.

These permissions are used only to provide app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To let you create, store, and share videos.
  • To personalize events, frames, and content.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal and security obligations when required.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only when necessary to operate the service, such as with infrastructure, storage, and authentication providers, or when required by law.

5. Storage and Security

Your data may be stored on secure third-party infrastructure used to provide the service, such as database, authentication, and cloud storage providers. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app. When your account is deleted, your personal data and associated content may be permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

8. Children

SpinShot 360 is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend reviewing this page regularly for changes.
